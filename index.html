Welcome

Experience outdoor living at its finest

Nestled among ancient forests and pristine lakes, MYS Retreat offers a one-of-a-kind camping experience where rustic charm meets modern comfort. Whether you prefer sleeping under the stars in a tent, bringing your RV, or cozying up in one of our handcrafted cabins, you'll find your perfect escape here.

Experience

Adventure Awaits

From sunrise hikes to evening campfires, every moment at MYS Retreat offers a chance to reconnect with the natural world. Our carefully curated activities cater to all ages and adventure levels.

Hiking Trails

Over 15km of marked trails ranging from easy nature walks to challenging summit hikes.

Lake Activities

Swimming, fishing, kayaking, and paddleboarding on our pristine private lake.

Night Sky Viewing

Minimal light pollution makes for incredible stargazing. Telescopes available.

Wildlife Watching

Spot deer, foxes, owls, and over 100 species of birds in their natural habitat.
