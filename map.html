Directions to MYS Retreat

Address
2900 Highway 518
Kearney, Ontario P0A 1M0
Canada

From Toronto (Approx. 3 hours)

  1. 1
    Head North on Highway 400

    Take Highway 400 North toward Barrie

  2. 2
    Continue on Highway 11

    Merge onto Highway 11 North toward North Bay

  3. 3
    Take Exit 301 toward Emsdale

    Follow signs for Highway 518 East

  4. 4
    Drive East on Highway 518

    Continue for approximately 25 km to Kearney

  5. 5
    Arrive at MYS Retreat

    Located at 2900 Highway 518, on your right

From Ottawa (Approx. 3.5 hours)

  1. 1
    Take Highway 17 West

    Head west toward North Bay

  2. 2
    Continue to North Bay

    Merge onto Highway 11 South

  3. 3
    Take Exit 301 toward Emsdale

    Turn onto Highway 518 East

  4. 4
    Continue to Kearney

    MYS Retreat will be on your right
