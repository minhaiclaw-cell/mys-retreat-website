Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about your stay at MYS Retreat family campground

Booking & Check-in

Check-in is from 2:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request — please contact us in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.

You can make a reservation through our online booking form, by calling us at +1 (249) 888-6668, or by emailing info@mysretreat.com. We recommend booking in advance, especially for weekends and holidays.

Yes, we require a 50% deposit at the time of booking to secure your reservation. The remaining balance is due upon arrival. For bookings during peak season (July-August), full payment may be required in advance.

Yes, you can modify your reservation dates up to 14 days before your scheduled arrival, subject to availability. Date changes within 14 days of arrival may incur a fee. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes.

Policies

Cancellations made 14 days or more before check-in receive a full refund. Cancellations 7-13 days before receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 7 days before are non-refundable, but we can offer a credit for a future stay valid for one year from the original booking date.

Yes! We're pet-friendly. Pets must be kept on a leash at all times and never left unattended. Please clean up after your pet. There is a $15/night pet fee per pet (maximum 2 pets per site). Certain aggressive breeds may be restricted — please contact us in advance.

Smoking is permitted only in designated outdoor areas and at your campsite. Smoking is strictly prohibited inside all cottages and RV rentals. Please dispose of cigarette butts properly and be mindful of fire hazards.

Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM. We ask all guests to be considerate of their neighbors during these times. Amplified music and loud gatherings should be concluded by 10:00 PM. Campfires may continue during quiet hours but should be kept at a respectful volume.

We do not offer refunds for inclement weather. However, if severe weather (such as a severe storm warning) makes camping unsafe, we will work with you to reschedule your stay or provide a credit for a future visit.

Amenities

All sites include a fire pit with grill grate, picnic table, and access to our modern washroom facilities with hot showers. RV sites include water and electrical hookups (30/50 amp). Cottages come fully furnished with kitchenettes, bedding, and private bathrooms.

Yes, we offer complimentary WiFi at the main lodge and common areas. RV sites also have WiFi access. Please note that due to our remote location, speeds may be slower than you're used to in the city. We encourage guests to unplug and enjoy nature!

Yes, we have coin-operated laundry facilities available on-site. The laundry room is open from 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily. Detergent is available for purchase at the camp store.

Yes! Our camp store sells firewood, ice, basic groceries, camping supplies, and snacks. Firewood is $8 per bundle or 3 for $20. During peak season, we recommend purchasing firewood early in the day as supplies can run low.

Yes, we have a sanitary dump station available for RV guests. The dump station is conveniently located near the entrance and is free for guests staying with us. Non-guests may use the dump station for a $20 fee.

Activities

Absolutely! Every campsite has its own fire pit. Firewood is available for purchase at our general store. Please check for any fire bans during dry conditions. Fires must be attended at all times and completely extinguished before leaving or going to sleep.

Yes! Himbury Lake has crystal clear water and is safe for swimming. There's a shallow sandy entry perfect for families with young children. We recommend water shoes as there are some rocks. Life jackets are available to borrow free of charge.

Yes, an Ontario fishing license is required for anyone 18-65 years old fishing in provincial waters. Licenses can be purchased online through the Ontario government website or at local retailers in Kearney. We can provide information on where to purchase licenses nearby.

Yes, we have kayaks and canoes available for rent. Rates are $25 for a half day (4 hours) or $40 for a full day. Life jackets and paddles are included. Advance reservations are recommended, especially on weekends. We also offer sunset paddle tours on Saturday evenings.

Yes! We have over 5 km of marked trails on our property winding through the pine forest. Additionally, we're just a short drive from Algonquin Provincial Park, which offers world-class hiking trails ranging from easy walks to challenging backcountry routes.

Winter at MYS Retreat is magical! Activities include snowshoeing on our trails, ice fishing on Himbury Lake (when ice conditions permit), cross-country skiing, and cozy campfires. Our cottages are fully winterized and heated for a comfortable stay.
